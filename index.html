THE GOAL: It's a 1v1 physics battle! Use your Compass Ball to knock the white ball into the recessed goal pockets at either end of the field.

PRO MOVES:
• Directional Jump: Launch where you aim!
• Flip Reset: Hit the ball with your engine (back) for a jump/boost reset!
• Air Strafe: nudge yourself left/right while in the air.
